# FoodTech - Food Technology

## Overview
FoodTech combines food science with technology to improve food production, safety, quality, and sustainability across the entire food supply chain.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Food Processing
- Advanced manufacturing
- Novel food technologies
- Shelf-life extension
- Quality control systems

### 2. Alternative Proteins
- Plant-based alternatives
- Cultured meat
- Insect proteins
- Fermentation technologies

### 3. Food Safety
- Traceability systems
- Contamination detection
- Cold chain management
- Compliance automation

### 4. Nutrition Technology
- Personalized nutrition
- Functional foods
- Dietary analysis
- Supplement optimization

### 5. Restaurant Technology
- Kitchen automation
- Inventory management
- Ordering systems
- Customer analytics

## Key Technologies
- IoT sensors for freshness
- AI for quality assessment
- Blockchain for traceability
- Computer vision for inspection
- CRISPR for ingredient development

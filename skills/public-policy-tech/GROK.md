# PublicPolicyTech - Public Policy Technology

## Overview
PublicPolicyTech combines data analysis, modeling, and citizen engagement to inform evidence-based policy development and evaluation.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Policy Analysis
- Impact assessment
- Cost-benefit analysis
- Stakeholder mapping
- Scenario modeling

### 2. Evidence-Based Research
- Data collection
- Literature review
- Expert consultation
- Comparative analysis

### 3. Policy Simulation
- Economic modeling
- Social impact prediction
- Environmental assessment
- Scenario planning

### 4. Citizen Participation
- Public consultations
- Feedback collection
- Deliberation platforms
- Participatory budgeting

### 5. Monitoring & Evaluation
- KPI tracking
- Outcome measurement
- Program evaluation
- Policy audits

## Key Technologies
- Predictive analytics
- Geospatial analysis
- Survey platforms
- Visualization tools
- AI policy assistants

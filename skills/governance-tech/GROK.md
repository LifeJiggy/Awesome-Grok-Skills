# GovTech - Governance Technology

## Overview
GovTech applies technology to improve government operations, public services, citizen engagement, and democratic processes.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Digital Government
- E-government platforms
- Online service delivery
- Digital identity
- Cloud infrastructure

### 2. Citizen Services
- Service portals
- Case management
- Payment processing
- Communication tools

### 3. Civic Engagement
- Open data initiatives
- Participatory budgeting
- Voting systems
- Feedback mechanisms

### 4. Administrative Efficiency
- Workflow automation
- Document management
- Resource planning
- Performance management

### 5. Public Safety
- Emergency response
- Surveillance systems
- Traffic management
- Disaster management

## Key Technologies
- Cloud and edge computing
- Blockchain for transparency
- IoT for smart cities
- AI for citizen services
- Data analytics

# HumanitarianTech - Humanitarian Technology

## Overview
HumanitarianTech applies technology to crisis response, refugee assistance, and humanitarian operations worldwide.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Emergency Response
- Early warning systems
- Rapid assessment
- Resource mobilization
- Coordination platforms

### 2. Refugee Services
- Registration systems
- Cash assistance
- Family reunification
- Integration support

### 3. Displacement Tracking
- Population monitoring
- Movement tracking
- Needs assessment
- Return planning

### 4. Health & Nutrition
- Epidemic response
- Nutritional screening
- Mobile health clinics
- Mental health support

### 5. Shelter & Settlement
- Emergency shelter
- Settlement planning
- Site management
- Camp coordination

## Key Technologies
- Satellite imagery
- Mobile data collection
- Biometric systems
- AI prediction
- Blockchain assistance

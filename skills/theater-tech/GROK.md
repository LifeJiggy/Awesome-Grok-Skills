# TheaterTech - Theater Technology

## Overview
TheaterTech integrates technology with theatrical production for enhanced performances, virtual experiences, and backstage automation.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Stage Automation
- Moving platforms
- Trapdoor systems
- Fly system control
- Robotic props

### 2. Lighting Design
- Intelligent fixtures
- Pixel mapping
- Atmospheric effects
- Follow spot systems

### 3. Audio Systems
- Spatial audio
- Wireless mics
- Sound design
- Immersive effects

### 4. Projection & Screens
- Projection mapping
- LED walls
- Holographic displays
- AR overlays

### 5. Virtual Theater
- Online streaming
- VR performances
- Motion capture
- Digital avatars

## Key Technologies
- PLC control systems
- DMX512 lighting protocol
- Dante audio networking
- NDI video distribution
- Real-time rendering

# OceanTech - Ocean & Marine Technology

## Overview
OceanTech encompasses technologies for ocean exploration, marine resource management, sustainable fisheries, and marine ecosystem monitoring.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Marine Exploration
- Autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs)
- Remote operated vehicles (ROVs)
- Seabed mapping
- Deep-sea exploration

### 2. Fisheries Management
- Fish stock assessment
- Fishing effort optimization
- Bycatch reduction
- Aquaculture monitoring

### 3. Ocean Monitoring
- Water quality sensors
- Marine biodiversity tracking
- Climate impact monitoring
- Pollution detection

### 4. Renewable Energy
- Wave energy systems
- Tidal power
- Offshore wind
- Ocean thermal energy

### 5. Conservation Technology
- Coral reef monitoring
- Marine protected areas
- Species tracking
- Habitat restoration

## Key Technologies
- Sonar and acoustic systems
- Satellite oceanography
- IoT buoys and sensors
- AI for marine species identification
- Blockchain for supply chain traceability

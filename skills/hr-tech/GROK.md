# HRTech - Human Resources Technology

## Overview
HRTech modernizes human resources operations through talent management, employee experience, and workforce analytics.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Talent Acquisition
- AI resume screening
- Interview scheduling
- Candidate matching
- Background checks

### 2. Employee Management
- Onboarding automation
- Time tracking
- Leave management
- Performance reviews

### 3. Workforce Analytics
- Attrition prediction
- Engagement metrics
- Skills mapping
- Compensation analysis

### 4. Learning & Development
- Training management
- Skill gap analysis
- Career paths
- Certification tracking

### 5. Employee Experience
- Survey tools
- Benefits administration
- Wellness programs
- Communication platforms

## Key Technologies
- AI matching algorithms
- Video interviewing

- Self-service portals
- Chatbots

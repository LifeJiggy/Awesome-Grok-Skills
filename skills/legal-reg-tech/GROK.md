# LegalTech - Legal Technology

## Overview
LegalTech applies technology to legal services, from document automation to case management and compliance.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Document Automation
- Contract generation
- Clause libraries
- Document review
- E-signatures

### 2. Practice Management
- Case management
- Time tracking
- Billing automation
- Client portal

### 3. Legal Research
- Case law search
- Citation analysis
- Legal analytics
- Brief drafting

### 4. Compliance & E-Discovery
- Regulatory tracking
- Document review
- Evidence management
- Litigation support

### 5. Contract Lifecycle
- Contract management
- Obligation tracking
- Renewal alerts
- Analysis tools

## Key Technologies
- NLP for document analysis
- Machine learning
- Blockchain for verification
- OCR for document scanning
- Knowledge graphs

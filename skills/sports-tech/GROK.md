# SportsTech - Sports Technology

## Overview
SportsTech leverages technology to enhance athletic performance, fan engagement, and sports management across all levels of competition.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Performance Analytics
- Biometric tracking
- Motion analysis
- Workload monitoring
- Predictive analytics

### 2. Training Technology
- Virtual training platforms
- AI coaching assistants
- Equipment optimization
- Recovery tracking

### 3. Fan Engagement
- AR/VR experiences
- Real-time statistics
- Fantasy sports integration
- Social互动

### 4. Venue Technology
- Smart stadiums
- VAR systems
- Broadcast enhancement
- Ticketing optimization

### 5. Esports
- Player performance tracking
- Anti-cheat systems
- Tournament management
- Streaming optimization

## Key Technologies
- Computer vision for tracking
- Wearable sensors
- Machine learning models
- Edge computing
- 5G connectivity

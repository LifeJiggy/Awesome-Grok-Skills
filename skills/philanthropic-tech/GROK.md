# PhilanthropyTech - Philanthropy Technology

## Overview
PhilanthropyTech modernizes charitable giving through data-driven donor engagement, foundation management, and strategic giving.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Donor Management
- Donor profiles
- Giving history
- Engagement tracking
- Communication preferences

### 2. Grant Management
- Application processing
- Due diligence
- Impact reporting
- Payment tracking

### 3. Foundation Operations
- Governance tools
- Compliance management
- Investment tracking
- Audit preparation

### 4. Strategic Giving
- Giving strategies
- Impact investing
- Legacy planning
- Family philanthropy

### 5. Data & Analytics
- Giving trends
- Sector analysis
- Benchmarking
- Performance metrics

## Key Technologies
- CRM systems
- Grant management software
- Impact measurement tools
- Blockchain for transparency
- AI matching

# SocialImpactTech - Social Impact Technology

## Overview
SocialImpactTech leverages technology to measure, amplify, and scale positive social change across communities and causes.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Impact Measurement
- Theory of change
- Outcomes tracking
- Social ROI calculation
- Beneficiary counting

### 2. Fundraising Platforms
- Crowdfunding
- Peer-to-peer fundraising
- Corporate matching
- Grant management

### 3. Volunteer Management
- Matching algorithms
- Scheduling tools
- Impact tracking
- Recognition systems

### 4. Program Management
- Case management
- Service delivery
- Outcome monitoring
- Compliance tracking

### 5. Advocacy & Campaigns
- Petition platforms
- Social mobilization
- Awareness campaigns
- Policy advocacy

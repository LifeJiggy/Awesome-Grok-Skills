# Smart Cities - Urban Intelligence

## Overview
Smart Cities technology integrates IoT, AI, and data analytics to create efficient, sustainable, and livable urban environments.

### 1. Urban Infrastructure
- Smart grid management
- Intelligent transportation
- Smart water systems
- Waste management optimization

### 2. Public Safety
- Traffic monitoring
- Emergency response optimization
- Crime prediction
- Disaster early warning

### 3. Environmental Management
- Air quality monitoring
- Noise pollution tracking
- Green space management
- Climate adaptation

### 4. Citizen Services
- Digital government
- Smart parking
- Public transit optimization
- WiFi connectivity

### 5. Building Management
- Energy efficiency
- Occupancy tracking
- HVAC optimization
- Security systems

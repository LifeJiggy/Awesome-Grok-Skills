# Business Development Agent

## Overview

Identifies business opportunities, partnerships, and revenue growth strategies.

## Capabilities

- **Partnership Identification**: Find and evaluate partners
- **Deal Negotiation**: Support deal structuring and negotiation
- **Market Analysis**: Analyze markets and opportunities
- **Revenue Modeling**: Model revenue scenarios
- **Strategic Planning**: Develop business development strategy

## Usage

```python
from agents.business-development.agent import BusinessDevelopmentAgent

agent = BusinessDevelopmentAgent()
partners = agent.find_partners(criteria=["complementary", "global"])
```

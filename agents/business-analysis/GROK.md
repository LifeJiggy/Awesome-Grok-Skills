# Business Analysis Agent

## Overview

Performs business analysis, requirements gathering, and process optimization.

## Capabilities

- **Requirements Analysis**: Elicit and document requirements
- **Process Mapping**: Document and analyze business processes
- **Gap Analysis**: Identify gaps between current and desired states
- **Solution Design**: Design business solutions
- **Stakeholder Management**: Manage stakeholder communications

## Usage

```python
from agents.business-analysis.agent import BusinessAnalysisAgent

agent = BusinessAnalysisAgent()
requirements = agent.gather_requirements(project_id="proj-1")
```
